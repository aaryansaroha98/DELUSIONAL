What this book is

A manifesto for anyone who was ever told
their dreams were too big.

The world has never been changed by people who thought small. DELUSIONAL is a short, sharp challenge to think bigger, believe before there's proof, and build a life too extraordinary to be ignored — written for the doubted, the underestimated, and the quietly ambitious.

What's inside

Three ideas that rewire how you think.

01

Think bigger than feels reasonable

Small goals are safe and forgettable. Learn why the "unrealistic" dream is often the only one worth chasing — and how to hold it without flinching.

02

Believe before the proof

Certainty arrives after you act, not before. Build the conviction to move while everyone's still waiting for evidence that you can.

03

Build an unshakable mindset

The world quietly expects you to stay small. This is the daily practice of refusing — turning doubt, from others and yourself, into fuel.

About the author

Aaryan Saroha

Aaryan Saroha is an entrepreneur and an Electrical Engineering undergraduate at IIT Jammu. He believes the world has never been changed by people who thought small.

His philosophy is simple: the people who are remembered were often called delusional before they were called successful. Through this book, he challenges readers to think bigger, believe before there's proof, and build a life too extraordinary to be ignored.
